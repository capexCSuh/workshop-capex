This page is for modules in the area of "Security beyond Computer Science. We are developing four modules in this area. The Beyond Computer Science team consists of: Professors Ping Chen (University of Massachusetts - Boston),  Farrukh Khan (Texas Southern University), Ernst Leiss (University of Houston), Wei W. Li (Texas Southern University) and Carlos Ordonez (University of Houston).
